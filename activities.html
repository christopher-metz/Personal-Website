Activities

I grew up very active, and I try to stay active as best I can now. I generally ski a lot in the winters but probably not much this year. This course will take up a lot of time. During the summer I enjoy surfing. There is actually a small but strong surf community in Washington. The most common place to surf in Washington is Westport. It's a small town on the coast west of Olympia. My favorite spot, although it is not consistantly good, is twin rivers. It's about 10 to 15 minutes west of Port Angeles on the Strait of Juan de Fuca. I also enjoy playing golf in the summers, and I play basketball year round. Camping and hiking I enjoy, but it is generally just used for the other activities.

A surfing pig!
